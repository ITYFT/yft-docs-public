## Mobile PWA Version

Your Trading Platform, Now Just a Tap Away

Access the full power of your trading platform directly from your home screen - no app store required. The Progressive Web App (PWA) version is optimized for mobile, giving you a smooth, app-like experience with all the core features: charting, trading, statistics, and more.

### Why Add It to Your Home Screen?

- Launch instantly like a native app
- Full-screen mode
- Offline-friendly: Some features work even without internet
- Always up to date – no manual updates needed
- Keeps you logged in

### How to Add to Home Screen

Here’s how to install the app on your device in seconds:

**On iOS (Safari)**

1. Tap the Share button in the Safari toolbar (bottom of screen)
2. Scroll down and select Add to Home Screen
3. Confirm the name (e.g. “Your Platform”) and tap Add

You’ll now see the platform icon on your home screen. Tap it anytime to launch directly into fullscreen trading.

**On Android (Chrome)**

1. Visit the trading platform in Chrome
2. You’ll see a banner that says "Add to Home Screen" - tap it
3. 
      - If not, tap the 3-dot menu (top-right)
      - Select Add to Home Screen
4. Confirm and tap Add

It’ll be added just like any other app, and opens independently of the browser.

## Account Metrics

![AD_4nXcuq-toigKGpsUvZ_bUs8DoSXEHzbJmQVHa8U4eIoPxLZdhLiZEHwNvlzHsk_2Ao5Ov33Itx32K-TWb6mebhSc4RcxUgii_Ih2UjuJDLqJZki8OgNn-JH85Qwlik9ZMomCG5FlSQw](./images/3c2285cf0d9f6b8c94a83f9784d59437.png)

The Account Metrics Panel gives you a real-time overview of your financial status at a glance - right at the top of the Markets tab. It’s built for clarity, speed, and decision-making on the go.

**What You See**

Split into two blocks for easy reading:

**Account Summary**

- Balance
  Your total funds excluding open trade PnL.
  → Updated after trades close or funds move.
- Equity
  Your real-time account value.
  → Formula: Balance + Unrealized PnL
- PnL
  The live profit/loss from open trades.
  → Color-coded: red = loss, green = profit

**Margin Status**

- Margin
  Amount of capital locked for open positions.
- Free Margin
  What’s available to open new trades or absorb losses.
  → Formula: Equity - Margin
- Margin Level (%)
  The ratio of equity to margin - critical for managing risk.
  → Formula: (Equity / Margin) × 100
  
  A high margin level = strong cushion.
  A low one can trigger margin calls.

### Why It Matters

- Stay aware of your exposure while browsing markets.
- Act fast - metrics update in real time.
- Manage risk smarter on mobile.

**Realized PnL / Balance Metrics**

Two main boxes:

- Realized PnL (USD): Shows profit/loss over the selected time frame.
- Balance (USD): Shows current account balance over time.

Both blocks offer Week/Month toggle options to switch time views.

**Balance Curve**

A line chart visualizing account Balance over time with selectable ranges:

- Day
- Week
- Month
- Year

Data updates as the account card changes.

**Realized PnL Chart**

Line chart showing Realized PnL fluctuations over time using a red/green line plot. Selectable views:

- Day
- Week
- Month
- Year

Useful for identifying trading performance trends.

**Multiple Accounts**

- Users can swipe left/right on the Account Card to switch between different accounts.
- Alternatively, tap the account dropdown on the top to search, filter, or switch between Active, Disabled, and Archived accounts.
