# FAQ

## Can I change the time zone on my platform?

Yes, you can change the time zone to match your preferences. Navigate to the **Settings** section, locate the **Time Zone** option, and select your desired time zone. This will adjust the display times across the platform, including charts and trading history.

---

## Where do price quotes come from?

Price quotes on the platform are sourced from a network of reliable liquidity providers, including banks and institutional market makers. These providers ensure accurate, real-time pricing for all instruments available on the platform.

---

## Where can I view my trading history?

Your complete trading history, including closed positions, pending orders, and transaction details, can be found in the **Trading History(Statistics)** section. This section is accessible from the main menu and provides filters to help you search specific dates or instruments.

---

## What happens to my open positions when I log out?

Your open positions remain active even when you log out of the platform. They will continue to fluctuate based on market movements. Take Profit (TP) and Stop Loss (SL) levels, if set, will function automatically. You can log back in anytime to monitor or manage your positions.

---

## Can I trade cryptocurrencies?

Yes, the platform supports cryptocurrency trading. You can trade a wide range of cryptocurrencies, including Bitcoin (BTC), Ethereum (ETH), and many more. These instruments are available 24/7, offering flexibility and opportunities outside traditional market hours.
